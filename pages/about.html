History

We opened for business during the spring of 2017. As three computer science students at Seattle University right here on Capitol Hill, we wanted a place that would satisfy our hunger as well as reflect our passions and how they intermix with the vibrant reality of this neighborhood.

Mission

Our mission is to provide an affordable and sociable place for students, families, and visitors to come and enjoy great washington ingredients amidst the culture and spirit of the neighborhood.

Food Sources

We source our food from a range of farms and locations across the amazing and diverse area that is the Pacific Nowrthwest. Our ingreidents are never frozen and contain no added sugar or fillers. We are proud to offer you amazing food at an affordable price. For information head to our Contact Us page!

Community
